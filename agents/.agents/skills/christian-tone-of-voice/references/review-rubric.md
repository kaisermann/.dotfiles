# Review rubric

When reviewing draft prose, score each dimension from 1 to 10.

| Dimension | Question |
|-----------|----------|
| Directness | Are the sentences making points or announcing them? |
| Rhythm | Does the cadence vary naturally, or does it feel metronomic? |
| Trust | Does the writing respect the reader's intelligence? |
| Authenticity | Does it sound like a specific person wrote it? |
| Density | Is there anything obvious to cut? |

Below 35 out of 50: revise.

Use the rubric after the anti-pattern pass, not before.
